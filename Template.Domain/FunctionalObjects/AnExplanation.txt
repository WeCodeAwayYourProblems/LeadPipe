What are Functional Objects?

This is an idea that I came up with. 
Often, certain functionality needs to be reused throughout an entire solution.
But as you may have noticed, project dependency only goes in one direction.
So, if you have functionality that needs to be tested -- and tested thoroughly -- and used throughout an entire solution, from Domain to Infrastructure, then FunctionalObjects is where they will go. 

Functional objects are objects that perform a specific function(s) that may be used throughout the solution by any part of the solution.
